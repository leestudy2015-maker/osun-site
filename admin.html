Headless CMS integration guide

When you are ready to automate updates, follow the quick plan below or share it with your developer.

  1. Connect a headless CMS such as Sanity, Contentful, or Strapi and recreate the hero, category, and about models that mirror the forms above.
  2. Replace the demo fetch in assets/js/content.js with live API calls, then hydrate the pages using the same data attributes.
  3. Use the export JSON action as your migration seed and schedule webhooks so content saves trigger a redeploy or cache refresh.

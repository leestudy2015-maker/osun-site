Founder

Meet Madam Liyana Osman

Raised in Klang, Madam Liyana blended her Peranakan heritage with contemporary styling to help women feel confident at every milestone. She believes beauty is a ritual of care, and each Osun appointment honours that ritual.

“When a woman feels seen, her glow is unstoppable. Osun exists to light that glow.”

15+ years curating Southeast Asian designers.

Certified beauty therapist and wardrobe stylist.

Hosts monthly glow circles for the Osun community.

Milestones

How Osun blossomed

From humble beginnings to a beloved destination boutique, we continue to evolve with our community.

2010 · Living room fittings

Madam Liyana hosted intimate styling sessions for friends, testing fabrics and silhouettes tailored to Malaysian weather.

2015 · Boutique opening

Osun’s first storefront launched in Bayu Tinggi, combining fashion racks with a beauty treatment room.

2023 · Glow collective

We introduced glow circles, wellness workshops, and digital styling appointments for global clients.
